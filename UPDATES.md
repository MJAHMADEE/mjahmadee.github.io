## 🎨 Design System

### Color Palette
- Primary: Blue (#3b82f6)
- Secondary: Purple (#a855f7)
- Accent: Pink, Green, Orange (for different sections)
- Neutral: Gray shades for text and backgrounds

### Typography
- Headings: Bold, gradient when needed
- Body: Regular weight, 14-16px
- Small text: 12-13px

### Spacing
- Section padding: py-20
- Container padding: px-6 md:px-16 lg:px-32
- Component gap: 4px to 8px
- Section separation: 24px (space-y-24)

### Icons
- All from lucide-react
- Consistent sizing (6-24px)
- Color-coded by section
- Smooth animations on hover

