Short Biography

Born in 1996 in Sari, Iran, Mohammad Javad Ahmadi earned his Diploma from NODET in 2015, his B.Sc. from Amirkabir University in 2019, M.Sc. from KNTU in 2022, and is now a Ph.D. candidate under Prof. Hamid D. Taghirad. Alongside academic pursuits, he serves as CTO at MediversAI & SmarTeeth and leads the ARAS AI, VR, and Software Group. His main interests include Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, and Computer Vision.
